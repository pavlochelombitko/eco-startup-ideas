Startup Ideas

Ecology-focused ventures with real impact potential

01

GreenBite — AI-Powered Food Waste Reducer

A mobile app that connects restaurants, supermarkets, and bakeries with consumers to sell surplus food at a discount before it goes to waste. Uses AI to predict daily surplus and optimize pricing dynamically. Think "Too Good To Go" but smarter — with meal-planning suggestions and carbon footprint tracking per purchase.

Food Waste AI / ML Marketplace
02

EcoTrace — Personal Carbon Footprint Dashboard

A web platform that connects to your bank account (via open banking APIs) and automatically categorizes your spending into carbon impact categories — transport, food, fashion, energy. Provides weekly "eco scores," personalized reduction challenges, and community leaderboards to gamify sustainable living.

Carbon Tracking FinTech Gamification
03

UrbanRoots — Micro-Farm Management SaaS

A platform for urban dwellers who want to grow food on rooftops, balconies, and community gardens. Offers IoT sensor integration (soil moisture, light, temperature), AI-driven planting calendars, and a marketplace to sell or share surplus harvest locally. Perfect for growing the urban agriculture movement in Central Europe.

Urban Agriculture IoT SaaS
04

CircleWear — Fashion Circular Economy Platform

A peer-to-peer platform specifically for renting, swapping, and reselling clothing within university communities. Each item has a "lifecycle score" showing how many times it's been reused, CO₂ saved, and water preserved. Partnerships with local designers turn irreparable clothes into upcycled limited collections.

Circular Economy Sustainable Fashion P2P
05

WattShare — Community Energy Trading

A blockchain-based platform that allows neighbors with solar panels, small wind turbines, or battery storage to sell excess energy directly to others in their community. Smart contracts automate fair pricing based on real-time grid demand. Targets Central European housing cooperatives and small municipalities.

Blockchain Renewable Energy Community

💡 Why ecology + entrepreneurship?

The EU Green Deal aims to make Europe climate-neutral by 2050, unlocking €1 trillion in sustainable investments. Ecology-focused startups are not just good for the planet — they're positioned at the centre of the biggest economic transformation of our generation. The SFF START Program is your launchpad.

06

BioPackHub — Compostable Packaging Marketplace

A B2B marketplace connecting small and medium businesses with verified compostable and biodegradable packaging suppliers across Europe. Includes a comparison tool for cost, decomposition time, certification status, and carbon impact. Helps SMEs transition away from single-use plastic without the research overhead.

Zero Waste B2B Marketplace Supply Chain
07

RiverWatch — Citizen Science Water Quality Monitor

An affordable, open-source water quality sensor kit paired with a crowdsourced data platform. Citizens place sensors in local rivers and streams; the platform aggregates data into real-time pollution maps. Targets environmental NGOs, municipalities, and schools. Revenue from premium analytics dashboards and B2G data licensing.

Hardware + SaaS Citizen Science Water Quality
08

GreenCommute — Corporate Mobility Credits

A platform for companies to incentivize green commuting. Employees log eco-friendly trips (cycling, public transit, carpooling) and earn credits redeemable for perks, days off, or donations to environmental causes. Companies get ESG reporting dashboards. Targets EU firms facing mandatory sustainability reporting under CSRD.

Green Transport ESG / CSRD HR Tech
09

SoilBank — Regenerative Agriculture Carbon Credits

A fintech platform that helps small farmers in Central & Eastern Europe earn income by adopting regenerative practices (cover cropping, reduced tillage, composting). SoilBank verifies soil carbon sequestration via satellite imagery and soil sampling, then sells verified credits to corporations. Farmers get paid, companies offset — everyone wins.

Carbon Credits AgriTech FinTech
10

EduWild — Gamified Biodiversity Education

A mobile app that turns nature walks into interactive learning adventures. Users scan plants and animals with their camera (AI-powered species recognition), collect "discovery cards," complete regional biodiversity quests, and contribute to open-source species databases. Monetized through school licenses, eco-tourism partnerships, and premium content packs.

EdTech Biodiversity Computer Vision
11

RepairMap — Right-to-Repair Local Network

A hyperlocal platform connecting consumers with independent repair shops, community fix-it events, and spare parts suppliers. Includes repair guides, difficulty ratings, and cost estimates for electronics, appliances, furniture, and clothing. Aligns with the EU's Right to Repair Directive and reduces e-waste at scale.

Circular Economy E-Waste Reduction Local Marketplace
12

CleanAir Collective — Indoor Air Quality as a Service

A subscription service providing smart air quality monitors and AI-driven ventilation recommendations for offices, schools, and co-working spaces. Dashboard shows real-time PM2.5, CO₂, VOC levels alongside health impact scores and energy-efficient improvement suggestions. Revenue from hardware rental + monthly SaaS subscriptions.

IoT / SaaS Air Quality Health Tech
